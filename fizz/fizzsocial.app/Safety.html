OUR PLATFORM IS CUSTOM ENGINEERED TO DELIVER A SAFER SOCIAL EXPERIENCE

To start, we believe that the safest online communities involve only verified members. Everyone who joins Fizz must verify that they are an active student of their school before gaining access to anything in the community.


Each Fizz community is actively monitored by a team of well-trained community moderators that ensure that all content strictly follows our Community Guidelines. No one moderator can remove a post without consensus from other moderators.
